Built on a modified version of Valve's Source engine, Garry's Mod (or GMod for short) is a physics sandbox game. One of the great strengths of Garry's Mod is that it is incredibly modder friendly. This has lead to a thriving community that is always coming up with new addons and gamemode for everyone to enjoy.

Features

With mod support and phsyics based build tools the only limitations in Garry’s Mod is your imagination.

Build

Create constructions with moving parts. Create a car and drive it around. Create a rocket and fly it to the moon.

Online co-op

You can play Garry’s Mod with your friends online, allowing you to cooperatively build together.

Addons

Garry’s Mod benefits from a rich productive community – so there’s always new addons to try.

Endless

There are a lot of gamemodes to play, and trying them is as easy as joining a server.
